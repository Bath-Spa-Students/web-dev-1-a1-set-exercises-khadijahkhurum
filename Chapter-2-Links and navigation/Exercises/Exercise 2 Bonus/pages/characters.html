Characters

  • Walter White: A high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer.
  • Skyler White: Walter's wife, who becomes entangled in his criminal activities.
  • Jesse Pinkman: Former student of Walter, involved in the drug trade.
  • Hank Schrader: DEA agent and Walter's brother-in-law.
  • Marie Schrader: Hank's wife, known for her interest in purple color and shoplifting.
