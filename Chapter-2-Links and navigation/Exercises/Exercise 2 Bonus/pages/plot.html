Plot

Breaking Bad is an American television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. It originally aired on the AMC network from January 20, 2008, to September 29, 2013. The series is a crime drama that revolves around the transformation of high school chemistry teacher Walter White into a ruthless drug lord.

The show's plot follows Walter White as he partners with former student Jesse Pinkman to produce and distribute high-quality methamphetamine. As Walter becomes more deeply involved in the drug trade, he faces moral dilemmas, personal challenges, and the constant threat of law enforcement.

Breaking Bad explores themes of morality, consequences, and the impact of choices on individuals and their families. The series is known for its intense and suspenseful storytelling, complex characters, and unexpected plot twists.
