My Favourite Recipe

This is a simple and delicious recipe that you can make at home. It only takes a few minutes to prepare and is perfect for a quick and easy desert to have. The following recipe is what I follow to make a fluffy vanilla sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the creamed mixture, then gradually add the dry ingredients.
  5. Stir in the milk and baking soda.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 9x9-inch pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
